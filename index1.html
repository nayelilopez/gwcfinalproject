Our Mission

Our mission for Water Warriors is to educate the youth with problems in our world that are less well known.
We want to spread a message that water all around the world is being polluted and there aren't many people taking action.
With our project, we hope to share a message worldwide that anyone can help end water pollution whether it's by volunteering
with others to clean waterway.
Even if you're busy and can't physically volunteer, you can raise awareness to a specific ocean or river
that is polluted but hasn't been cared for.
We want our users to be informed about water pollution but also be encourage to help the water.
